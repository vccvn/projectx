The 24h News Editorial Board

The editorial board is composed of 10 journalists with wide-ranging areas of expertise. Their primary responsibility is to write 24h News’s editorials, which represent the voice of the board, its editor and the publisher.

The board is part of the 24h News’s editorial department, which is operated separately from the 24h News’s newsroom, and includes the Letters to the Editor and Op-Ed sections.

Nelson Palmer, Editor

@NPalmer

Nelson Palmer, the editorial page editor of 24h News, is in charge of the Opinion department. He oversees the editorial board.

Mr. Palmer became editorial page editor in June 2016. He is a senior assignment editor who oversees national and international coverage. His mandate is to dream up interesting approaches to the news of the day.

Stacy Parks, Corespondent

Stacy Parks joined 24h News in 2001. She is a television and digital correspondent for 24hNews. She covers the companies and business leaders that influence everyday life and unravels the complexities of Wall Street and corporate America.

Stacy has also brought her brand of investigative reporting to the U.S. industrial food complex with a recently released documentary-style series.

Walter Thornton, Executive VP of Programming

Walter Thornton joined 24h News in the fall of 1997. He is 24h News's Executive Vice President of programming. In this role, he oversees all New York- and Atlanta-based live programs, including mornings, dayside, prime time, and weekends. In addition, he manages network-wide booking, sponsored content and special projects.

Before joining the 24h News, he worked as a freelance journalist and parliamentary aide in London, writing for different publications and doing research work for members of Parliament.

Deborah King, Senior Staff Editor

Deborah King has been an editor at the Times editorial page since 1998, beating the arrival of the Internet by years. In addition to helping produce and edit 24h News's daily editorial coverage, she also reports on IT developments at several civilian-facing federal agencies.

Before coming to 24h News, she was a reporter and copy editor at E&E Publishing, and an assistant producer and editorial assistant at NPR.

Kelvin Floyd, News Editor

Donald French, Senior Enterprise Reporter

Donald French, who joined the editorial board in 2004, has worked for 24h News since 1993. He is a senior enterprise reporter for 24h News Politics, covering the 2016 presidential campaign and politics across the United States and around the world.

Mr. French is an Emmy award-winning journalist whose experience spans more than three decades.
